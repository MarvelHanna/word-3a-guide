Step 3 — 10 pts

Outline edit on Slide 2

  1. View → Outline View.
  2. Slide 2: click the last bullet. Home → Paragraph → Indent Less.
  3. Click at the end of that bullet's text. Press Return for a new bullet.
  4. Home → Paragraph → Indent More.
  5. Type or paste:
Snorkeling equipment is provided
  1. View → Normal.
Step 4 — 6 pts

Slide 2 line spacing 1.5

  1. Click into the content placeholder on Slide 2. Cmd+A.
  2. Home → Paragraph → Line Spacing → 1.5.
Step 5 — 10 pts

Slide 1 layout → Title Slide

  1. Click Slide 1.
  2. Home → Slides → Layout → Title Slide.
Step 6 — 10 pts

Subtitle formatting

  1. Triple-click the subtitle Hikes and Rafting.
  2. Home → Font: Cambria, size 24.
  3. Font Color arrow → Theme Colors → first color in the second column.
  4. Cmd+B (Bold), Cmd+I (Italic).
Step 7 — 10 pts

Copy 3 slides from p01F_Slides

  1. Open p01F_Slides.pptx in another PowerPoint window.
  2. In its thumbnails, click the first of the last 3 slides, Shift-click the last. Cmd+C.
  3. Switch back to your Hanna_PowerPoint_1F_Hawaii_Tours.pptx.
  4. In thumbnails, click Slide 3.
  5. Right-click between Slide 3 and Slide 4 → Paste Options → Use Destination Theme.
Three new slides land after Slide 3 in the same order they were in the source.
Step 8 — 10 pts

Slide 4: center title and caption

  1. Click Slide 4.
  2. Click into the title placeholder. Cmd+A, Cmd+E.
  3. Click into the caption placeholder. Cmd+A, Cmd+E.
Step 9 — 8 pts

Slide Sorter: delete + move

  1. View → Slide Sorter.
  2. Right-click Slide 3 → Delete Slide.
  3. The old Slide 4 is now Slide 3. Drag it to between Slide 1 and Slide 2 (so it becomes the new Slide 2).
  4. View → Normal.
Step 10 — 6 pts

Move Slide 4 to end

  1. In thumbnails, drag Slide 4 to the very end of the deck.
Step 11 — 10 pts

Split transition, Horizontal Out, 1.75s, all slides

  1. Click Slide 1.
  2. Transitions → Split.
  3. Effect Options → Horizontal Out.
  4. Duration: 01.75.
  5. Apply To All.
Step 12 — 10 pts

Notes & Handouts header/footer

  1. Insert → Text → Header & Footer.
  2. Click the Notes and Handouts tab.
  3. Check Date and time → Update automatically.
  4. Check Page number.
  5. Check Footer. Type or paste:
1F_Hawaii_Tours
  1. Apply to All.
