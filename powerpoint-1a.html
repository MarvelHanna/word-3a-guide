1.03 — 10 pts

Apply Berlin theme

  1. Design → Themes → More (arrow at right of gallery).
  2. Hover thumbnails to read ScreenTips. Click Berlin.
  3. If Berlin isn't shown: Browse for Themes → navigate to the assignment folder → pick Berlin.thmx → Apply.
  4. If the Designer pane opens, close it.
1.04 — 17 pts

Insert Slides 2 and 3

  1. Home → Slides → New Slide arrow → Panoramic Picture with Caption. Slide 2 appears.
  2. In the title placeholder (below the picture), type:
Your Dreams
  1. In the text placeholder under the title, paste this caption:
Whether you want to stroll through a medieval village or spend your day shopping and dining in a vibrant city, Quest for Adventure Travel can make your dream a reality.
  1. Home → Slides → New Slide arrow → Title and Content. Slide 3 appears.
  2. Slide 3 title:
Our Expertise
  1. Slide 3 content (first bullet):
Over 20 years of experience in the travel industry.
